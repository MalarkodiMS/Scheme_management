How it works?

      Our platform is on the basis of communicating between the people and the government about the people's problem.

      The people's problem can be solved by conveying their messages to the respective government officials.

      If the respective goverment officials are not responding to their duties then the people can able to complaint about their problem by facing the government officials with the proof attached.

      Their problem has been now transfered to the superior of that government official both their own problem and by the goverment officials side.

      They can easily follow up to their respective schemes that they have applied (or) registered for.

Who are all related?

      Though it is a platform between the government officials and people so , all the respective govenment officials are related.

Why they are related?

      Goverment officials are related to solve the problem faced by the people (or) the problem raised by the prespective government official.
